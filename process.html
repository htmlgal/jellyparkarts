Process

Here is an overview of my artistic process.

Step 1: Style

Before starting any sort of piece, I pick what type of style I want to use. Do I want to create a realistic portrait? Do I want to illustrate a character?


For this piece, I chose to go for a more messy style imitating a guache painting. I used the 'wet marker' brush on Procreate.

Step 2: Sketching

I roughly sketch out what I want to draw. I try not to waste time working on small parts of the piece that are bound to change.


I like to use a transparent brush that I can layer with, in case I want to add shadows.


Time: 28 minutes

Step 3: Base

I add general blocks of color to the drawing, again leaving out the small details that I'll refine later.

Time: 12 minutes

Time elapsed: 40 minutes

Step 4: Render and Refine

I add more specific details with smaller brushes and more intricate colors. This is my favorite part, because I get to watch the painting come together and really appear as I envisioned it.

For this step, I use smaller brushes.

Time: 1 hr 50 minutes

Time elapsed: 2 hr 30 minutes

Step 5: Finalize

I make sure my painting is proportional and meets my standards. I sign the painting, then export it as a png.


This painting took a total of 2 hours and 30 minutes.


Typical finish times for my paintings are:

  • Digital: 30 min-4 hours
  • Acrylic: 3-6 hours
  • Watercolor: 1-2 hours
