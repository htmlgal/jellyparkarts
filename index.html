Jelly Park

18 year old artist based in the Bay Area, CA


- 10 years of artistic experience

- CSSSA 2018 alumna

- PTA Reflections Contest 2019 - Award of Merit

- 2020-2021: Graphic designer for The Teen Magazine


In 2018, as a freshman in high school I attended CSSSA (California State Summer School of the Arts) and learned about figure drawing, painting, and multimedia. Through this summer program, I made connections with other talented teen artists and improved my artistic skills.


In 2019, I won the Award of Merit in my local PTA's Reflections contest. I created a painting based on the academic pressures high school students in the Silicon Valley face.


I currently attend Penn State as a student pursuing Computer Science. Though art isn't as large of a part of my life as I want it to be, I sketch in my free time.


I intend on pursuing art as a hobby as I further my academic career.
