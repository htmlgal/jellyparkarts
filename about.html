About

I am a Bay Area based artist who creates primarily digital art, though my favorite mediums are acrylic paint and watercolor. I love drawing people and capturing humanity's candid moments. I began my art journey at the age of 5, and have been drawing for 10 years. Throughout my entire life, I have used art as a medium to express myself. Every emotionally charged moment in my life, I have documented through visual arts, writing, or music. Art is very important to me and I hope it always remains a part of my life.


In my free time, I draw, play the piano, and hang out with friends.
